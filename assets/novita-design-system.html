01

Visual Identity

Three words anchor every decision below. When a choice is unclear, the one that is more fresh, more grounded, or more verified wins.

Fresh

Alive, not clinical

Green leads. Light pale-green paper, generous air, produce-real color. Never spa-pastel, never sterile white.

Grounded

Earthy & honest

Clay warmth and confident weight. Premium through restraint and material color, not gloss or gradient tricks.

Verified

Earned trust

Monospaced labels borrow the authority of a nutrition panel. Data is precise. The check mark means we proved it.
